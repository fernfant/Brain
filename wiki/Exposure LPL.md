# Exposure LPL (Leads Per Listing)

## Description
A metric measuring how many leads each listing receives — a proxy for ROI delivered to agents and property portals. Tracked separately for Buy and Rent segments; Q2 2026 targets were reset after conflict-driven demand collapse.

## Appearances
- [[sources/wbr-jan-monthly-lookback]] — Buy eLPL at 8.3 (vs 8.9 YoY); Rent stable at 14.9
- [[sources/wbr-week-12]] — Buy eLPL: 3.7 (vs 7.6 target) — -51% leads; Rent eLPL: 7.1 (vs 11 target) — -43% leads; Q2 targets to be reset
- [[sources/wbr-week-13]] — Both segments remain below target
