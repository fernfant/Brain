# Share of Voice (SoS / SoV)

## Description
Metric tracking Property Finder's share of organic search traffic (SoS — Share of Sessions) or keyword visibility (SoV — Share of Voice) relative to competitors, segmented by channel (SEO, App, Web) and vertical (Buy, Rent, Generic).
